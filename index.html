What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of making machines think and act like humans. It allows computers to understand, learn, and make decisions automatically.

AI in Daily Life

  • Voice assistants like Alexa and Google Assistant
  • Face recognition on smartphones
  • Recommendation systems in Netflix and YouTube
  • Self-driving cars
  • AI-powered chatbots in customer support

AI Applications

  • Healthcare: AI helps in diagnosing diseases and recommending treatments.
  • Education: AI can create personalized learning experiences.
  • Finance: AI detects fraud and predicts stock market trends.
  • Robotics: AI powers robots to perform complex tasks safely.
  • Smart Homes: AI controls devices like lights, AC, and security systems.

Misuse of AI

While AI can be very helpful, it can also be misused. Here are some examples:

  • Deepfakes: AI can create fake videos or images of people saying or doing things they never did.
  • Privacy Issues: AI can track personal data without permission, like your location or habits.
  • Bias in Decisions: AI may make unfair decisions if trained on biased data (e.g., in hiring or loans).
  • Automation of Jobs: Some jobs may be replaced by AI, causing unemployment in certain areas.
  • Cybersecurity Threats: AI can be used to create smarter malware or hacking tools.
